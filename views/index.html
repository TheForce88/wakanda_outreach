About Us

WAKANDA: the Kingdom of Wakanda, is a small isolationist landlocked country located in Africa, surrounded by mountain ranges and a thick forest. It is ruled by King T'Challa, the heir of the mantle of Black Panther, and is the only known source of the metal vibranium.

OUTREACH: As part of our information sharing initiave, Wakanda as built a number of Outreach Centers across the globe. Nakia Baily oversees the social outreach, and Shuri will spearhead the Science and Information Exchange.

Powered by Vibranium

Vibranium was first deposited on Earth by a meteorite 10,000 years ago. Wakandan Vibranium is the most common variety, and is often referred to simply as "vibranium". It is a rare substance native only to the small African nation of Wakanda. The isotope possesses the ability to absorb all vibrations in the vicinity as well as kinetic energy directed at it. The energy absorbed is stored within the bonds between the molecules that make up the substance.
